Highlights

On the Westside near lots of shops and restaurants

Brash roasts their own coffee beans every Thursday and can ship

Offers a selection of baked goods and pastries

Details

$4 Latte, Average

Free at Westside Provisions District parking deck

Wifi available but not much seating inside

Located inside a repurposed shipping container

"This is such a unique spot, not just because it's built inside a shipping container. They also roast their own coffee beans, which you can buy in bulk inside and online. While there isn't much seating (or standing) space inside, that's ok. There are a ton of shopping and dining options all within walking distance. Grab an Americano and walk around Westside Provisions."

– Grant

"BRASH lives up to it's name. It's a bold little coffee shop serving some high-quality locally roasted beans. I'm more of a latte girl myself, but at Brash I ordered a decaf Americano and it was tasty and full of flavor. The atmosphere is hip and while the inside is small, it has plentiful outdoor wooden seating if it's a nice day. They just opened a second location at the Atlanta History Center in Buckhead!"

– Elaine
