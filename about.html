About the Project

ATL Coffee Map started as an idea and then became a reality after Grant and Elaine moved to the Old Fourth Ward in Atlanta. They love exploring the city and trying different places to eat and drink, so on the weekends they decided to start going to a new coffee shop each week. The ritual of discovering a new place, trying a latte and tasty croissant became a special time they look forward to each week. After doing this for a few weeks they thought that there should be a better and easier way to discover the great local, small coffee shops around the city. So ATL Coffee Map was born as a way to share their passion and help others discover new coffee shops hidden around our fine city.

The Explorers

Grant Sadowski

Creator, Developer, Photgrapher

Favorite drink: Americano

Grant has been calling Atlanta his home since 2003 and while he’s moved away twice, he keeps coming back. By day, Grant is a project manager for an engineering company; by night, he’s a newbie front end web developer with equal interest in product management and design. His passion is creating digital experiences that not only excite people but push expand his understanding of what web development can be. Grant can often be found running around the city on foot preparing for his next big race, hanging out in coffee shops with Elaine or exploring the ever-changing Atlanta food scene.

Elaine Stephenson

Creator, Designer, Photographer

Favorite drink: Chai Tea Latte

An Atlanta native and UGA graduate, Elaine is a full-time designer, artist and chai tea latte lover. From her design background and love of crafting by hand, she finds her calling in painting and hand-lettering. Her ultimate creative outlet is discovering ways to combine these two passions into beautiful art. When she is not creating art or lettering everyday, she is often practicing yoga, exploring Atlanta with Grant and stealing cuddles from their rescue kitty Cleo.
